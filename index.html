About

Mr. Y. Venkateswara Rao is a senior advocate with over 26 years of distinguished legal practice, specializing in civil, criminal, property, and corporate law. Since beginning his legal career in 1998, he has been consistently representing clients before the Hon'ble Amaravati High Court and various District Courts across Andhra Pradesh. Known for his strategic thinking, in-depth legal expertise, and a strong commitment to client success, Mr. Rao has built a trusted reputation for delivering practical legal solutions tailored to each client's needs. His career is driven by professionalism, integrity, and a client-first approach. With decades of courtroom experience and a proven record of achieving positive outcomes, Y. Venkateswara Rao continues to guide individuals, families, and businesses through complex legal matters with trusted counsel and effective representation.
